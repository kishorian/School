About Sanghamitra Vidyalayam

Our Legacy

Since 1979, Sanghamitra Vidyalayam has been at the forefront of educational excellence in Nellore. For over 45 years, we have been shaping young minds and building future leaders.

Our Vision

To nurture young minds into well-rounded individuals who excel academically and embody strong moral values.

Our Mission

At Sanghamitra School, we are committed to:

  • Providing holistic education that empowers students
  • Fostering critical thinking and creativity
  • Building character through values-based education
  • Creating a supportive learning environment
