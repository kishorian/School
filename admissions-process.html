Admission Process 2025-26

A Step-by-Step Guide to Joining Sanghamitra School

Admission Steps

Step 1: Application Form

Obtain and fill the application form:

  • Download from website
  • Collect from school office
  • Office timing: 9:00 AM to 3:30 PM

Step 2: Document Submission

Submit the following documents:

  • Completed application form
  • Birth Certificate
  • Previous school TC (for classes II onwards)
  • Progress Report Card
  • 4 passport size photographs
  • Aadhar Card copy
  • Parent's ID proof

Step 3: Interaction

School interaction process:

  • Meeting with Principal/Management
  • Student assessment (if applicable)
  • Parent interaction

Step 4: Admission Confirmation

Upon selection:

  • Receive admission confirmation
  • Fee payment details
  • Class allocation

Age Criteria (as of June 2025)

  • LKG: 3+ years
  • UKG: 4+ years
  • Class 1: 5+ years
  • Other classes: Age appropriate to the class

Important Guidelines

  • Admission is subject to vacancy availability
  • All documents must be submitted in original with one set of photocopies
  • Incomplete applications will not be processed
  • The school reserves the right of admission
  • Fee once paid is non-refundable

Admission Timeline

  • Application Process Starts: December 2024
  • Document Verification: Within 3 working days of submission
  • Interaction Schedule: Will be communicated after document verification
  • Final Admission: Subject to successful completion of all steps
